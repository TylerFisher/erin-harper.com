Erin Harper

Erin Harper is a scriptwriter, multimedia producer, editor, podcast host and photographer. She currently writes, hosts and produces the Ambie-nominated podcast 12 Years That Shook the World, from the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, where she has worked since 2015. Prior to joining the Museum, Harper was a Multimedia Editor at the Chicago Tribune from 2006 to 2015.
